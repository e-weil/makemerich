# MakeMeRich

**Know Your Worth.**

How rich are you, really? Enter your income and find out where you stand
among 8 billion humans. See your earnings per second, compare yourself
to billionaires, and discover what your money buys around the world.

**Try it:** [makemerich.app](https://makemerich.app)

## Features

- **Global Ranking** — see what percentile you're in worldwide
- **Money Clock** — watch your earnings tick up in real-time
- **Billionaire Comparison** — how long for Elon Musk to earn your salary?
- **Purchasing Power** — you're poor in Switzerland, rich in Vietnam
- **What It Buys** — your salary in Big Macs, Teslas, and Bitcoin
- **Wealth Card** — a beautiful shareable image with your stats
- **Daily Tips** — one actionable money tip per day

## How It Works

No AI. No API calls. No signup. No data stored.

Just math. HTML + CSS + JavaScript. Everything runs in your browser.

Enter your annual income. Get perspective. Share your Wealth Card.

## Quick Start

```bash
git clone https://github.com/e-weil/makemerich.git
cd makemerich

# Just open it
open app/index.html

# Or serve it
cd app && python -m http.server 8080
# Open http://localhost:8080
```
